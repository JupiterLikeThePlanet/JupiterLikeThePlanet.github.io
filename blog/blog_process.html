Looking Back

October 18th 2015

How was the process?

I've enjoyed my time utilizing HTML and CSS to construct a site. I've seen my knowledge build over time, mostly getting comfortable with the basics. It's been rocky trying to incorporate javascript and jQuery, though. That's a challenge I'm hoping to amelliorate once we start DBC. As for CSS, I'm having fun manipulating attributes. On my main page, I was able to change stock images like this one:

... and change them into custom buttons I've created using the 'div' tag and css:


Blog


It is through these exercises that I see the merge of science and art. Every element is like a puzzle piece of design and a developer must figure out where the pieces go and how they fit on a page. That's what keeps me interested. I still feel a bit sloppy at the moment. Sort of like a kid learning how to walk or draw, but hasn't developed the motor skills to do either smoothly. Often times, I feel like I'm just plopping something on the page (compared to someone who knows how to do this) and it could be smoothed out if I just had a bit more practice. I look forward to diving deeper into all the stuff I've just scratched the surface of.
